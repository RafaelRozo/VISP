VISP LEVEL 3 — CERTIFIED PROFESSIONAL TERMS AND CONDITIONS
Version 1.0 — Effective Date: January 1, 2026

THESE TERMS AND CONDITIONS ("LEVEL 3 TERMS") SUPPLEMENT THE INDEPENDENT
CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT AND PLATFORM TERMS OF SERVICE. BY ACCEPTING A
LEVEL 3 DESIGNATION, YOU AGREE TO THE FOLLOWING ADDITIONAL TERMS.

1. LEVEL 3 — CERTIFIED PROFESSIONAL DESIGNATION
1.1. Level 3 Certified Professionals perform licensed, regulated, and
     specialized services that require government-issued trade licenses,
     professional certifications, and mandatory insurance coverage.
1.2. Examples of Level 3 tasks include, but are not limited to:
     - Licensed electrical work (ESA-regulated in Ontario)
     - Licensed plumbing (including backflow prevention)
     - HVAC installation, repair, and maintenance
     - Gas fitting (TSSA-regulated in Ontario)
     - Structural carpentry and renovation
     - Roofing (commercial and residential)
     - Licensed pest control
     - Asbestos and hazardous material abatement
     - Fire alarm and sprinkler system installation
     - Pool and spa installation and maintenance

2. MANDATORY QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS
2.1. All Level 1 and Level 2 qualification requirements must be met.
2.2. Valid, current provincial or state trade license(s) applicable to
     the services you intend to perform through the Platform. You must
     provide:
     (a) License number and issuing authority;
     (b) Jurisdiction of validity;
     (c) Expiry date (if applicable);
     (d) Scanned copy of the license for verification.
2.3. VISP will verify your license directly with the issuing authority.
     Providing a fraudulent, expired, or invalid license will result
     in immediate and permanent removal from the Platform and may be
     reported to the relevant regulatory body.
2.4. All applicable certifications required by your jurisdiction
     (e.g., Certificate of Qualification, Red Seal endorsement).
2.5. Criminal Record Check (CRC) with Vulnerable Sector Screening
     obtained within the last six (6) months.

3. MANDATORY INSURANCE — $2,000,000 MINIMUM
3.1. Level 3 Certified Professionals MUST maintain commercial general
     liability insurance with a MINIMUM coverage amount of TWO MILLION
     CANADIAN DOLLARS ($2,000,000 CAD) or equivalent in USD.
3.2. The insurance policy must:
     (a) Be issued by a licensed insurer authorized to operate in your
         jurisdiction;
     (b) Name VISP Technologies Inc. as an additional insured;
     (c) Include completed operations coverage;
     (d) Cover the specific trades and services you perform;
     (e) Remain valid and in force at all times while you are active
         on the Platform.
3.3. You must provide proof of insurance (Certificate of Insurance)
     prior to activation and upon each renewal.
3.4. VISP will monitor insurance expiry dates. Failure to renew before
     expiry will result in automatic suspension of your account until
     valid proof of insurance is provided.
3.5. You must notify VISP within forty-eight (48) hours of any
     insurance policy cancellation, material change, or claim.

4. COMPENSATION
4.1. Level 3 Certified Professionals are compensated at rates ranging
     from $90.00 to $150.00 CAD per hour, as determined by the Platform
     based on the trade, specific task, geographic region, licensing
     requirements, and demand conditions.
4.2. Complex or specialized tasks may command rates at the higher end
     of the range or above, subject to VISP approval.

5. COMMISSION STRUCTURE
5.1. VISP charges a commission of eight percent (8%) to twelve percent
     (12%) on each completed Level 3 job.
5.2. The reduced commission rate reflects the higher qualification
     requirements and insurance costs borne by Level 3 providers.
5.3. The specific commission rate for each job is displayed before
     you accept the task.

6. REGULATORY COMPLIANCE
6.1. You are solely responsible for ensuring that all work performed
     complies with applicable:
     (a) Building codes (Ontario Building Code, National Building Code
         of Canada, or applicable US codes);
     (b) Electrical safety codes (Ontario Electrical Safety Code, NEC);
     (c) Fire codes and safety standards;
     (d) Environmental regulations;
     (e) Occupational health and safety legislation (OHSA in Ontario).
6.2. You are responsible for obtaining or advising the customer to
     obtain all necessary permits before commencing work.
6.3. You must ensure all work passes required inspections by the
     relevant authority having jurisdiction (AHJ).

7. PROFESSIONAL LIABILITY
7.1. In addition to general liability insurance, Level 3 Providers
     performing design, engineering advisory, or consulting services
     are strongly recommended to carry professional liability
     (errors and omissions) insurance.
7.2. You agree to indemnify and hold harmless VISP from any claims,
     damages, penalties, or fines arising from your work, including
     code violations, failed inspections, or regulatory non-compliance.

8. QUALITY STANDARDS
8.1. All work must meet or exceed the standards required by the
     applicable regulatory authority and trade association.
8.2. You must maintain a minimum customer rating of 4.2 out of 5.0
     stars averaged over your last forty (40) completed jobs.
8.3. You must maintain a task completion rate of at least ninety-five
     percent (95%).
8.4. Workmanship is warranted for a period of one (1) year from
     completion, in addition to any statutory warranty periods.

9. CONTINUING EDUCATION
9.1. You must maintain all continuing education and professional
     development requirements mandated by your licensing authority.
9.2. VISP reserves the right to require evidence of continuing
     education compliance on an annual basis.

10. SUSPENSION AND REMOVAL
10.1. VISP may immediately suspend your Level 3 status for:
      (a) Expired or revoked trade license;
      (b) Lapsed insurance coverage;
      (c) Failed inspection reported through the Platform;
      (d) Regulatory enforcement action;
      (e) Safety incident or complaint.
10.2. Suspension is effective immediately and remains in place until
      the matter is resolved to VISP's satisfaction.

INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT AND PLATFORM TERMS OF SERVICE. YOU
CONFIRM THAT YOU HOLD ALL REQUIRED LICENSES AND INSURANCE AS SPECIFIED
ABOVE.
