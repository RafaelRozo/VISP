VISP/TASKER LEVEL 2 — EXPERIENCED PROVIDER TERMS AND CONDITIONS
Version 1.0 — Effective Date: January 1, 2026

THESE TERMS AND CONDITIONS ("LEVEL 2 TERMS") SUPPLEMENT THE INDEPENDENT
CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT AND PLATFORM TERMS OF SERVICE. BY ACCEPTING A
LEVEL 2 DESIGNATION, YOU AGREE TO THE FOLLOWING ADDITIONAL TERMS.

1. LEVEL 2 — EXPERIENCED DESIGNATION
1.1. Level 2 Experienced Providers perform technical and skilled tasks
     that require demonstrable expertise, a professional portfolio, or
     specialized training, but do not require government-issued trade
     licenses or professional certifications regulated by law.
1.2. Examples of Level 2 tasks include, but are not limited to:
     - Advanced painting and finishing (interior and exterior)
     - Drywall patching, repair, and taping
     - Tile installation and grouting
     - Flooring installation (laminate, vinyl, hardwood)
     - Fence and deck repair (non-structural)
     - Fixture installation (shelving, blinds, curtain rods)
     - Appliance installation (non-gas, non-hardwired)
     - Smart home device setup and configuration
     - Pressure washing
     - Advanced landscaping and garden design

2. QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS
2.1. All Level 1 qualification requirements must be met and maintained.
2.2. Successful completion of a Criminal Record Check (CRC) obtained
     within the last twelve (12) months.
2.3. A professional portfolio demonstrating relevant experience. The
     portfolio must include a minimum of five (5) documented projects
     or equivalent evidence of competency.
2.4. A minimum of two (2) years of verifiable experience in the
     applicable trade or skill area.
2.5. Completion of the Tasker Level 2 skills assessment for each
     task category you wish to be qualified in.
2.6. Any non-mandatory but relevant certifications or training
     certificates are encouraged and will be noted on your profile.

3. COMPENSATION
3.1. Level 2 Experienced Providers are compensated at rates ranging
     from $60.00 to $90.00 CAD per hour, as determined by the Platform
     based on the specific task type, your qualifications, geographic
     region, and demand conditions.
3.2. Premium rates within the range may be available for providers
     with exceptional ratings and verified specialty certifications.
3.3. Rate adjustments may be made by VISP based on market conditions,
     with thirty (30) days' prior notice.

4. COMMISSION STRUCTURE
4.1. VISP charges a commission of twelve percent (12%) to eighteen
     percent (18%) on each completed Level 2 job.
4.2. The specific commission rate for each job is displayed before
     you accept the task.
4.3. Providers with consistently high ratings (4.5+ over 50 jobs)
     may qualify for reduced commission rates at VISP's discretion.

5. SCOPE LIMITATIONS
5.1. Level 2 Experienced Providers are strictly prohibited from
     performing any task that requires:
     (a) A provincial or state trade license (e.g., licensed
         electrician, licensed plumber);
     (b) Government-issued professional certification;
     (c) Structural modifications requiring an engineer's approval;
     (d) Work requiring a building permit, unless the permit has been
         obtained by the customer and the work does not require a
         licensed tradesperson;
     (e) Any task designated as Level 3 or Level 4 on the Platform.
5.2. If during the course of a job you discover that the work requires
     a licensed professional, you must immediately cease the affected
     work, inform the customer, and trigger a level escalation through
     the Platform.

6. INSURANCE
6.1. Level 2 Experienced Providers are strongly recommended to maintain
     commercial general liability insurance with a minimum coverage of
     $500,000 CAD.
6.2. Providers who maintain verified insurance coverage will receive a
     "Insured" badge on their profile and may receive priority in the
     matching algorithm.

7. TOOLS AND EQUIPMENT
7.1. You are responsible for providing your own tools and equipment
     necessary to perform Level 2 tasks.
7.2. All tools and equipment must be maintained in safe, working
     condition and comply with applicable safety standards (e.g.,
     CSA, UL).
7.3. VISP is not responsible for loss, damage, or theft of your tools
     or equipment.

8. QUALITY ASSURANCE
8.1. Level 2 work is subject to customer review and may be subject
     to random quality audits conducted by VISP or its designees.
8.2. You must maintain a minimum customer rating of 4.0 out of 5.0
     stars averaged over your last thirty (30) completed jobs.
8.3. You must maintain a task completion rate of at least ninety-two
     percent (92%).
8.4. You agree to warranty your workmanship for a period of thirty
     (30) days from task completion for defects attributable to your
     work.

9. ADVANCEMENT AND DEMOTION
9.1. Level 2 Providers who obtain required licenses and certifications
     may apply for advancement to Level 3 by submitting documentation.
9.2. Providers who consistently fail to meet Level 2 performance
     standards may be demoted to Level 1 or removed from the Platform.

BY ACCEPTING LEVEL 2 EXPERIENCED PROVIDER STATUS, YOU CONFIRM THAT YOU
HAVE READ, UNDERSTOOD, AND AGREE TO THESE TERMS IN ADDITION TO THE
INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT AND PLATFORM TERMS OF SERVICE.
