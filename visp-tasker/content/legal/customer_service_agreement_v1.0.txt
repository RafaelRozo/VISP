THIS CUSTOMER SERVICE AGREEMENT ("AGREEMENT") IS ENTERED INTO BETWEEN
VISP TECHNOLOGIES INC. ("VISP", "WE", "US") AND THE CUSTOMER ACCEPTING
THESE TERMS ("CUSTOMER", "YOU") UPON YOUR FIRST USE OF THE VISP
PLATFORM TO REQUEST SERVICES.

1. SERVICE REQUEST PROCESS
1.1. All service requests must be made through the VISP platform
     using the predefined task catalog. Free-text task descriptions
     are not available.
1.2. You will select the appropriate service category and task type
     that best matches your needs.
1.3. Upon submitting a service request, the Platform will match you
     with a qualified, verified independent service provider
     ("Provider") based on the task requirements, your location,
     Provider availability, ratings, and proximity.
1.4. You acknowledge that VISP does not employ the Providers and that
     VISP acts solely as a technology platform facilitating the
     connection between you and independent contractors.

2. PROVIDER LEVELS AND SERVICE QUALITY
2.1. The Platform classifies Providers into four levels based on their
     qualifications, certifications, and experience:
     - Level 1 (Helper): Basic household tasks, $25-45/hr
     - Level 2 (Experienced): Skilled tasks, $60-90/hr
     - Level 3 (Certified Pro): Licensed/regulated services, $90-150/hr
     - Level 4 (Emergency): 24/7 emergency services, $150+/hr
2.2. All Providers undergo background checks and qualification
     verification appropriate to their level before being activated
     on the Platform.
2.3. Level 3 and Level 4 Providers are required to hold valid trade
     licenses and maintain a minimum of $2,000,000 in commercial
     general liability insurance.

3. PRICING AND PAYMENT
3.1. The price for each service is determined by the Platform and
     displayed to you before you confirm the service request. The
     price is based on the task type, Provider level, estimated
     duration, geographic region, and current demand.
3.2. You agree to pay the quoted price plus any applicable taxes
     (HST/GST in Canada, state/local sales tax in the USA).
3.3. Payment is processed securely through Stripe. By using the
     Platform, you agree to Stripe's terms of service.
3.4. Your payment method will be charged upon completion of the
     service, unless otherwise specified for the service type.
3.5. For jobs estimated to exceed $500 CAD, a hold may be placed
     on your payment method at the time of booking.

4. EMERGENCY SERVICES AND DYNAMIC PRICING
4.1. Emergency services (Level 4) may be subject to dynamic pricing
     that reflects urgency, time of day, Provider availability, and
     demand conditions.
4.2. Dynamic pricing will be clearly displayed before you confirm
     an emergency service request. You must provide explicit consent
     to dynamic pricing before the request is processed.
4.3. VISP does not guarantee response times for emergency services
     but Providers are bound by Service Level Agreements that
     include specific response time targets.

5. SCOPE OF WORK
5.1. The scope of each job is defined by the task selected from the
     Platform catalog. Providers cannot unilaterally expand the scope.
5.2. If the Provider identifies additional work beyond the original
     scope, a new service request must be created through the Platform.
5.3. You should not ask or allow a Provider to perform work outside
     the scope of the original task without creating a new job.

6. YOUR RESPONSIBILITIES
6.1. You must provide accurate information about the service location,
     access requirements, and any known hazards or conditions relevant
     to the task.
6.2. You must ensure the Provider has safe access to the work area.
6.3. You must be present (or have an authorized representative present)
     at the service location during the scheduled service, unless the
     service type permits otherwise.
6.4. You are responsible for obtaining any permits required for the
     work, unless the task specification states that permit acquisition
     is included in the service.

7. CANCELLATION POLICY
7.1. Cancellations made more than twenty-four (24) hours before the
     scheduled service: No charge.
7.2. Cancellations made between four (4) and twenty-four (24) hours
     before the scheduled service: A cancellation fee of twenty-five
     percent (25%) of the estimated job total may apply.
7.3. Cancellations made less than four (4) hours before the scheduled
     service or after the Provider has departed: A cancellation fee
     of fifty percent (50%) of the estimated job total may apply.
7.4. Emergency service cancellations are subject to the Emergency
     Pricing Consent terms.

8. REVIEWS AND RATINGS
8.1. After each completed service, you will be invited to rate the
     Provider and leave a review.
8.2. Reviews must be honest, fair, and based on your actual experience.
8.3. VISP reserves the right to remove reviews that contain abusive
     language, discriminatory content, or are determined to be
     fraudulent.

9. COMPLAINTS AND DISPUTES
9.1. If you are dissatisfied with a service, you may file a complaint
     through the Platform within seventy-two (72) hours of service
     completion.
9.2. VISP will investigate complaints and may offer resolution options
     including partial or full refunds, re-service by a different
     Provider, or account credits.
9.3. Disputes not resolved through the complaint process are subject
     to the dispute resolution provisions in the Platform Terms of
     Service.

10. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
10.1. VISP is a technology platform and does not perform the services.
      VISP is not liable for the acts or omissions of Providers.
10.2. Your sole remedy for unsatisfactory service is through the
      complaint and dispute resolution process described above.
10.3. VISP's total liability to you shall not exceed the amount you
      paid for the specific service giving rise to the claim.

11. PROPERTY ACCESS AND SECURITY
11.1. By requesting a service, you authorize the assigned Provider to
      enter your property for the purpose of performing the task.
11.2. VISP recommends that you secure valuables and sensitive items
      before a Provider arrives.
11.3. VISP is not responsible for loss or damage to your property
      except to the extent caused by a Provider's negligence, which
      is the Provider's liability as an independent contractor.

12. PRIVACY
12.1. Your personal information is collected, used, and protected in
      accordance with our Privacy Policy and applicable privacy
      legislation (PIPEDA, provincial privacy acts, and applicable
      US state privacy laws).
12.2. Your contact information is shared with the assigned Provider
      solely for the purpose of completing the requested service.
