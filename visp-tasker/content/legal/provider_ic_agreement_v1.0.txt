THIS INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT ("AGREEMENT") IS ENTERED INTO
BETWEEN VISP TECHNOLOGIES INC. ("VISP", "COMPANY") AND THE INDIVIDUAL
ACCEPTING THESE TERMS ("PROVIDER", "CONTRACTOR", "YOU").

RECITALS
WHEREAS VISP operates the Tasker platform, a technology marketplace
connecting customers with independent service providers; and WHEREAS
the Provider wishes to offer services through the Platform as an
independent contractor;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained
herein, the parties agree as follows:

1. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS
1.1. You are an independent contractor and NOT an employee, agent,
     partner, or joint venturer of VISP. Nothing in this Agreement
     creates an employment relationship.
1.2. You are solely responsible for determining the manner and means
     of performing the services, subject to the quality standards and
     requirements of the Platform.
1.3. VISP does not control the hours you work, the sequence of your
     tasks, or the tools you use beyond those required by applicable
     law and professional standards.
1.4. You retain the right to provide services through other platforms
     or directly to clients outside the Platform.

2. TAX OBLIGATIONS
2.1. As an independent contractor, you are solely responsible for:
     (a) Reporting all income earned through the Platform to the
         applicable tax authorities (Canada Revenue Agency, IRS, or
         provincial/state tax agencies);
     (b) Remitting all applicable income taxes, self-employment taxes,
         HST/GST, and any other taxes or levies;
     (c) Maintaining appropriate tax registration (e.g., GST/HST
         registration if applicable);
     (d) Filing all required tax returns on time.
2.2. VISP will issue T4A slips (Canada) or 1099 forms (USA) as
     required by law.
2.3. VISP does not withhold taxes from your earnings and has no
     obligation to do so.

3. PROVIDER OBLIGATIONS
3.1. You shall maintain all licenses, certifications, permits, and
     insurance required by applicable law for the services you perform.
3.2. You shall perform all accepted tasks in a professional, workmanlike
     manner and in compliance with all applicable laws, regulations,
     building codes, and industry standards.
3.3. You shall not subcontract or delegate any task accepted through
     the Platform without prior written approval from VISP.
3.4. You shall not solicit customers met through the Platform for
     off-platform services for a period of twelve (12) months following
     the last task completed for that customer.

4. SCOPE OF SERVICES
4.1. Services available through the Platform are defined by the closed
     task catalog. Providers may only perform tasks that match their
     verified qualifications and assigned level.
4.2. Providers CANNOT unilaterally expand the scope of a job. Any
     additional services require a new job to be created through the
     Platform.
4.3. If a Provider identifies work beyond the original job scope, they
     must inform the customer through the Platform and a new job must
     be requested.

5. LEVEL DESIGNATION AND QUALIFICATIONS
5.1. Providers are assigned a level (1 through 4) based on verified
     qualifications, experience, and certifications.
5.2. Level assignment determines which tasks a Provider can accept
     and the applicable compensation range.
5.3. Attempting to perform tasks above your designated level is a
     violation of this Agreement and may result in immediate removal
     from the Platform.

6. COMPENSATION AND COMMISSION
6.1. Compensation for each task is determined by the Platform based on
     the task type, provider level, region, and applicable pricing rules.
6.2. VISP deducts a commission from each completed job. Commission rates
     vary by level:
     - Level 1: 15-20%
     - Level 2: 12-18%
     - Level 3: 8-12%
     - Level 4: 15-25%
6.3. Payment is processed through Stripe and deposited to your linked
     bank account according to the payout schedule.

7. INSURANCE AND LIABILITY
7.1. You shall maintain adequate insurance coverage appropriate for your
     level and the services you perform.
7.2. Level 3 and Level 4 providers must maintain a minimum of $2,000,000
     (two million dollars) in commercial general liability insurance.
7.3. You are solely responsible for any damage, injury, or loss arising
     from the services you perform.
7.4. You agree to indemnify and hold harmless VISP from any claims
     arising from your performance of services.

8. BACKGROUND CHECKS
8.1. All Providers must consent to and successfully pass a Criminal
     Record Check (CRC) before being activated on the Platform.
8.2. VISP reserves the right to require additional background checks
     at any time.
8.3. Failure to pass a background check or refusal to consent to a
     check will result in removal from the Platform.

9. TERMINATION
9.1. Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time, for any
     reason, with or without cause.
9.2. Upon termination, you must complete any in-progress tasks unless
     otherwise directed by VISP.
9.3. VISP may immediately suspend or terminate your access to the
     Platform for violation of this Agreement, the Platform Terms of
     Service, or applicable law.

10. CONFIDENTIALITY
You shall not disclose any confidential information obtained through
the Platform, including customer personal information, proprietary
business processes, pricing algorithms, or internal communications.

11. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Province of Ontario
and the federal laws of Canada applicable therein.

12. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Disputes arising under this Agreement shall be resolved through binding
arbitration in Ontario, Canada, in accordance with the Arbitration
Act, 1991 (Ontario).

13. SEVERABILITY
If any provision of this Agreement is held to be unenforceable, the
remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

14. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This Agreement, together with the Platform Terms of Service and
applicable level-specific terms, constitutes the entire agreement
between you and VISP regarding your provision of services through
the Platform.

BY ACCEPTING THIS AGREEMENT, YOU ACKNOWLEDGE THAT YOU HAVE READ AND
UNDERSTOOD ALL OF THE ABOVE TERMS, AND THAT YOU ARE ENTERING INTO
THIS AGREEMENT VOLUNTARILY AS AN INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR.
