VISP/TASKER LEVEL 1 — HELPER TERMS AND CONDITIONS
Version 1.0 — Effective Date: January 1, 2026

THESE TERMS AND CONDITIONS ("LEVEL 1 TERMS") SUPPLEMENT THE INDEPENDENT
CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT AND PLATFORM TERMS OF SERVICE. BY ACCEPTING A
LEVEL 1 DESIGNATION, YOU AGREE TO THE FOLLOWING ADDITIONAL TERMS.

1. LEVEL 1 — HELPER DESIGNATION
1.1. Level 1 Helpers provide basic household and manual tasks that do
     not require specialized licenses, certifications, or technical
     training beyond general competency.
1.2. Examples of Level 1 tasks include, but are not limited to:
     - General cleaning and tidying
     - Furniture assembly (flat-pack)
     - Yard work and basic landscaping
     - Moving assistance and heavy lifting
     - Organizing and decluttering
     - Minor errand running
     - Basic painting (non-commercial)
     - Snow shovelling and seasonal tasks

2. QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS
2.1. Minimum age of eighteen (18) years.
2.2. Successful completion of a Criminal Record Check (CRC) obtained
     within the last twelve (12) months.
2.3. Valid government-issued photo identification.
2.4. Proof of legal authorization to work in Canada or the United
     States, as applicable.
2.5. Completion of the Tasker Level 1 orientation program, including
     the safety and customer interaction modules.

3. COMPENSATION
3.1. Level 1 Helpers are compensated at rates ranging from $25.00 to
     $45.00 CAD per hour, as determined by the Platform based on
     the specific task type, geographic region, and demand conditions.
3.2. You acknowledge that VISP sets the customer-facing price and that
     your compensation is the agreed rate minus the applicable commission.
3.3. Rate adjustments may be made by VISP based on market conditions,
     with thirty (30) days' prior notice.

4. COMMISSION STRUCTURE
4.1. VISP charges a commission of fifteen percent (15%) to twenty
     percent (20%) on each completed Level 1 job.
4.2. The specific commission rate for each job is displayed before
     you accept the task.
4.3. Commission rates may be adjusted with sixty (60) days' notice.

5. SCOPE LIMITATIONS
5.1. Level 1 Helpers are strictly prohibited from performing any task
     that requires:
     (a) A provincial or state trade license;
     (b) Electrical, plumbing, HVAC, or gas-fitting work;
     (c) Structural modifications to a building;
     (d) Work that requires a building permit;
     (e) Any task designated as Level 2, 3, or 4 on the Platform.
5.2. If you encounter a situation that exceeds Level 1 scope during
     a job, you must immediately stop the affected work, notify the
     customer, and report the situation through the Platform so that
     an appropriate escalation can occur.

6. INSURANCE
6.1. Level 1 Helpers are encouraged but not required to maintain
     personal liability insurance.
6.2. VISP provides a supplemental accidental damage coverage program
     for Level 1 jobs up to $5,000 CAD per incident, subject to the
     terms of that program.

7. PERFORMANCE STANDARDS
7.1. You must maintain a minimum customer rating of 3.5 out of 5.0
     stars averaged over your last twenty (20) completed jobs.
7.2. You must maintain a task completion rate of at least ninety
     percent (90%).
7.3. Falling below these thresholds may result in reduced job
     visibility, temporary suspension, or removal from the Platform.

8. ADVANCEMENT
8.1. Level 1 Helpers who obtain relevant certifications or licenses
     may apply for advancement to Level 2 by submitting documentation
     through the Platform.
8.2. Advancement decisions are made at VISP's sole discretion based
     on qualification verification, performance history, and platform
     needs.

9. TERMINATION OF LEVEL 1 STATUS
9.1. VISP may revoke your Level 1 designation at any time for cause,
     including but not limited to:
     (a) Violation of scope limitations;
     (b) Failing to meet performance standards;
     (c) Customer safety complaints;
     (d) Expiration of your background check without renewal.

BY ACCEPTING LEVEL 1 HELPER STATUS, YOU CONFIRM THAT YOU HAVE READ,
UNDERSTOOD, AND AGREE TO THESE TERMS IN ADDITION TO THE INDEPENDENT
CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT AND PLATFORM TERMS OF SERVICE.
